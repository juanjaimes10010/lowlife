Making Delicious Food Since 2016

Dennis B&K has been a delicious place to enjoy food in the Little Village area for over 25 years. The Family owned restaurant has been commited to serve classic Chicago pizza, hotdogs, beef, and a great selection of Mexican food. You can enjoy our food here at 3806 W.26th street in Chicago, Ill or call and order to deliver to you.

About Eliut Patraca

Eliut Patraca was born in november 18, 1969. Eliut was born and raised in Mexico by his Mother and Father. At the age of 18 he traveled to the United States in search for better opportunities. He arrived to Chicago,Ill in an area called Little Village where he has lived ever since. Eliut had a passion for cooking so he decided to work at a popular fast food restaurant in little village for the next 25 years. After learning more about the restaurant business he decided to leave his job and start his own restaurant in 2018. He decided to buy Dennis B&k which was originally called Dennis until 2018 when Eliut added B&k in respect for his 2 daughters Brianna and Kassandra.
